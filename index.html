Three-phase draft format
Phase 1
Snake draft — ranks 9–48
Randomized order, snake format. Each player picks their chosen number of underdog teams. No duplicates.
Phase 2
Top-4 pick — France, Spain, Argentina, England
Every player picks 1 team from ranks 1–4. Duplicates allowed — everyone gets a top team.
Phase 3
Top-8 pick — Portugal, Brazil, Netherlands, Morocco
Every player picks 1 team from ranks 5–8. Duplicates allowed.
